# Design Guidelines — SMPM2 Solo

## Identity
Islamic and Muhammadiyah institutional identity. The design should feel trustworthy, academic, and welcoming — suitable for a school that serves families and the broader community.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Green**: `#2E7D32` (dark green — Muhammadiyah/Islamic identity)
- **Dark Blue**: `#1A237E` (authority, trust, academic)
- **Gold / Accent**: `#F9A825` (warmth, highlight, Islamic motif accent)
- **Background Light**: `#F5F5F5`
- **White**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Text Dark**: `#212121`
- **Text Muted**: `#757575`

## Typography
- **Headings**: Poppins, Bold / SemiBold — modern yet formal
- **Body**: Inter or Noto Sans — clean, readable
- **Arabic/Islamic accent** (optional): decorative use only for section headers or motifs

## Layout & Spacing
- Mobile-first responsive layout
- Clean card-based sections
- Generous whitespace for readability
- Max content width: 1200px, centered

## Elevation & Shadows
- Subtle card shadows: `box-shadow: 0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`
- Navbar with slight shadow on scroll

## Components
- **Navbar**: Fixed top, school logo + name left, nav links right, hamburger on mobile
- **Hero Banner**: Full-width with school photo background, overlay, school name and tagline
- **Card Components**: Rounded corners (8–12px), white bg, subtle shadow
- **Buttons**: Primary = green fill, Secondary = outlined green or gold accent
- **Section Headers**: Centered, with decorative underline in gold
- **Footer**: Dark green or dark blue background, white text, links to all main sections
- **Announcement cards**: Clean list with date badge, title, short excerpt
- **Gallery**: Masonry or grid layout
- **Staff cards**: Photo, name, subject/position

## Islamic/Muhammadiyah Motifs
- Subtle geometric Islamic patterns as background accents (SVG or CSS)
- Sun/Muhammadiyah logo incorporated where appropriate
- Crescent or geometric motifs in section dividers (tasteful, not overdone)

## Mobile-First
- Touch-friendly tap targets (min 44px)
- Collapsible mobile nav
- Responsive image grids
- Readable font sizes on small screens (min 16px body)
