# SMPM2 Solo — School Website

## Product Overview
SMPM2 Solo is the official website for SMP Muhammadiyah 2 Surakarta, a Junior High School (SMP) located in Surakarta (Solo), Indonesia, under the Muhammadiyah organization.

## Target Users
- Parents (orang tua siswa)
- Prospective students (calon siswa baru)
- Current students and teachers
- General public / community

## Brand & Tone
- Formal, professional, and trustworthy
- Islamic and Muhammadiyah identity
- Warm and welcoming to families
- Language: Indonesian (Bahasa Indonesia) throughout

## Core Features / Pages
1. **Homepage** — Hero banner, school highlights, quick links, latest announcements preview
2. **Profil Sekolah** (School Profile)
   - Sejarah (History)
   - Visi & Misi
   - Akreditasi
   - Struktur Organisasi
3. **PPDB** (Penerimaan Peserta Didik Baru / New Student Enrollment)
   - Persyaratan pendaftaran (requirements)
   - Jadwal PPDB (schedule)
   - Prosedur pendaftaran (registration procedure)
4. **Pengumuman** (Announcements) — list and detail view; NO news/berita module
5. **Akademik** (Academic Info)
   - Kurikulum
   - Jadwal pelajaran
   - Kalender akademik
   - Ekstrakurikuler
6. **Prestasi** (Student Achievements)
7. **Galeri** (Photo Gallery)
8. **Guru & Staff** (Teachers & Staff directory)
9. **Kontak** (Contact page with Google Maps embed)

## Anti-References / Exclusions
- NO news/berita articles — only pengumuman (announcements)
- No e-learning or LMS features
- No student login/portal
- No online payment features

## Strategic Principles
- Mobile-first design
- Clear and easy navigation for non-tech-savvy parents
- Content in Bahasa Indonesia
- Reflect Islamic/Muhammadiyah values through design and content tone
